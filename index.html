Ice cream is one of the most popular frozen desserts enjoyed by people of all ages around the world. It is typically made from a mixture of milk, cream, sugar, and flavorings, which is churned and frozen to achieve a smooth and creamy texture. With countless flavors ranging from classic vanilla and chocolate to unique combinations like cookies and cream or mango delight, ice cream caters to a wide variety of tastes. It is often served in cones, cups, sundaes, or paired with cakes and waffles, making it a versatile treat for every occasion. Beyond its delightful taste, ice cream has become a symbol of happiness, celebration, and comfort, enjoyed during summer days, family gatherings, or simply as a small moment of joy.
